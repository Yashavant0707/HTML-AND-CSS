IPL WINNER'S TEAM

RAJSTHAN ROYALS

14 years after their maiden Indian Premier League (IPL) title win, Rajasthan Royals (RR) will aim to lift the trophy for the 2nd time when they take on Gujarat Titans in the final in Ahmedabad today (May 29). Under Shane Warne's leadership, RR had lifted the trophy back in 2008. They haven't played an IPL final since then.

DECCAN CHARGERS

Deccan Chargers Hyderabad (abbreviated as HDC) was a franchise cricket team based in the city of Hyderabad in the Indian Premier League. After finishing last in the first season of the IPL, they won the second season held in South Africa in 2009 under the captaincy of former Australian wicket-keeper and batsman Adam Gilchrist.

CHENNAI SUPER KINGS

Chennai Super Kings is a cricket franchise which makes its appearances in the Indian Premier League. The team is led by Mahendra Singh Dhoni and has reached the playoffs in 10 out of the 11 seasons they played. Chennai Super Kings have won 3 IPL Titles so far, and have been the Runners Up on 4 occasions.

KOLKATA KNIGHT RIDERS

KKR became the IPL champions in 2012, by defeating Chennai Super Kings in the final. They repeated the feat in 2014, defeating Kings XI Punjab.The Knight Riders hold the record for the longest winning streak by any Indian team in T20s.
